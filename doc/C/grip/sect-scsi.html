Using a SCSI drive

If you are using a SCSI drive, or an IDE drive under SCSI emulation, Grip needs to access the generic scsi device in addition to the cdrom device. On most systems, the generic scsi device will be /dev/sgx, where 'x' is a number. Unless you have multiple scsi devices, the device would be /dev/sg0. Ensure that your user account has access to this device, and that this device is specified in your rip configuration (see the Section called The "Rip" config tab in the chapter called Configuring Grip).
